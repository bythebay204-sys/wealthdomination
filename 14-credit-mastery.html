The Credit Mastery System

Credit is not a score. It is a capital access tool. This system shows you how to build, repair, and weaponize your credit profile — personal and business — so you can move on deals that require capital you haven't saved yet.

The 30/60/90-Day Plan

Days 1–30

Audit & Dispute

  • Pull all 3 bureau reports
  • Flag every inaccuracy
  • Send Round 1 dispute letters
  • Open secured card if needed
  • Reduce utilization below 30%

Days 31–60

Build & Stack

  • Follow up on disputes
  • Apply for first net-30 tradeline
  • Open business checking + EIN
  • Apply for Crown Office Supplies
  • Request CLI on existing cards

Days 61–90

Scale & Leverage

  • Apply for Brex or Ramp
  • Add 2 more net-30 accounts
  • Target 3 reporting tradelines
  • Apply for business line of credit
  • Review scores — all 3 bureaus

The Business Credit Stack

Step 1 — Foundation

Wyoming or Delaware LLC. EIN from IRS. Business checking account. Business address. DUNS number from D&B (free, takes 30 days). This is your business credit identity — without it, nothing else reports.

Step 2 — Net-30 Tradelines

Crown Office Supplies, Uline, Quill, Summa Office Supplies, Grainger. These vendors extend net-30 terms and report to D&B and Experian Business. Three reporting tradelines with on-time payments builds a Paydex score of 80 within 90 days.

Step 3 — Business Credit Cards

Brex (Day 1 — no personal guarantee for qualified entities). Ramp and Coast (Day 60). Divvy and BILL (Day 90). Each card adds a reporting tradeline, increases available credit, and builds the profile needed for bank lines.

Step 4 — Bank Lines & SBA

With 6-12 months of business banking history, consistent revenue, and a strong Paydex score, a $25,000–$250,000 business line of credit becomes accessible. This is the capital stack that funds deals without personal guarantee.

Start Your Credit Build Today

Three moves this week. The 90-day clock starts when you do.

  1. Pull your reports at AnnualCreditReport.com — all three bureaus, free.
  2. Register your LLC's DUNS number at dnb.com. Free. Takes 30 days to activate.
  3. Open an account with Crown Office Supplies using your EIN. Your first net-30 tradeline.
