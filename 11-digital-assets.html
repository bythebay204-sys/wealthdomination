Module 06 — The Billionaire Blueprint

Digital Assets & New Money

The internet created an entirely new asset class that didn't exist 30 years ago. Online courses, digital products, automated systems, and crypto-based instruments are generating generational wealth for people who understand them — and invisible losses for people who don't. This module covers both sides.

Digital Asset Categories

01

Digital Products & Courses

Zero marginal cost. Infinite scale. A course, guide, workbook, or template built once can sell indefinitely. The DMT International model is proof. This is the most accessible wealth vehicle for a solo operator.

02

Content & Audience Assets

A following is a distribution channel. A newsletter, a YouTube channel, a podcast — each one is an asset that generates attention, trust, and ultimately revenue. Built slowly, worth enormously.

03

Cryptocurrency & Tokens

Bitcoin as digital gold. Ethereum as programmable value. Altcoins as high-risk, high-reward speculation. Each plays a different role in a digital asset stack. Understand the difference before you allocate.

04

Automated Business Systems

A business that runs without your daily presence is a digital asset. Funnels, email sequences, payment processors, and delivery systems — when wired correctly — generate revenue around the clock.

Building Income That Runs Without You

The Funnel as an Asset

A properly built sales funnel is a machine. Traffic enters, value is delivered, a transaction occurs, a product is delivered automatically. The WealthPath funnel you're using right now is an example. This can be replicated across any niche.

Crypto Risk Management

Position sizing, cold storage, network selection, and exit strategy. Crypto rewards early, asymmetric positioning and punishes emotional, unplanned allocation. Never allocate more than you can afford to lose completely — and know your exit criteria before you enter.

The DMT International Model

Digital education products distributed through automated funnels, supported by business credit and entity structure, with revenue reinvested into hard assets. This is the model. You are inside it right now.

Affiliate & Licensing Income

Earn a percentage of other people's products. License your content, your brand, or your system to others. These are leverage income streams that require relationship and credibility — both of which compound over time.

Build Your Digital Stack

Three actions. Digital income starts with a single product.

  1. Identify one piece of knowledge or content you own that could be packaged as a digital product this week.
  2. Map a basic funnel: landing page → email capture → delivery page → upsell. Sketch it on paper first.
  3. Research one crypto position sized at no more than 2-5% of your liquid net worth. Know your thesis before you buy.
