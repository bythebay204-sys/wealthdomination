Module 07 — The Billionaire Blueprint

Protection & Legal Structure

Building wealth without a protection layer is building a house without a roof. One lawsuit, one audit, one bad partnership, one accident — and everything is exposed. This module builds the legal and structural architecture that keeps what you've built safe.

Core Protection Structures

01

The LLC

The foundational entity. Separates personal assets from business liability. Single-member, multi-member, series — each has a role. Every income-generating activity should live inside a properly structured LLC.

02

The Holding Company

A parent entity that owns the operating entities below it. Assets held at the top, operations running below. If one operating company gets sued, the holding company — and everything in it — is shielded.

03

Trusts

Revocable living trusts avoid probate. Irrevocable trusts protect assets from creditors. Land trusts anonymize real property ownership. Each trust has a specific job. Know which one you need and when.

04

Insurance Stack

Umbrella policy, professional liability, key man, and property coverage. Insurance is the cheapest protection layer. A $2M umbrella policy costs less than $500 per year and covers gaps that no entity structure can.

Building Wealth AND Keeping It

The Entity Stack

A Wyoming holding LLC owns a California operating LLC owns the real estate, the intellectual property, and the business accounts. This structure is legal, common among sophisticated operators, and creates layers of protection between what you build and those who might try to take it.

Asset Titling

How you title an asset determines what happens to it in a lawsuit, a divorce, or at death. Real property in a trust. Business interests in an LLC. Investment accounts with proper beneficiary designations. Titling is not a detail — it is a strategy.

The Offshore Question

Panama foundations, Nevis LLCs, and Cook Islands trusts are legitimate tools used by high-net-worth individuals for asset protection — not tax evasion. Understanding when and why to add an international layer is a decision for later stages of wealth building, but the framework should be understood early.

What They Never Taught You

The American legal system makes it easy to take money from individuals and far harder to take money from properly structured entities. The wealthy don't have better lawyers after the fact — they have better structure before the fact. This module gives you the same structure.

Lock Down Your Structure

Three moves to build your protection layer.

  1. Verify your LLC is active and in good standing in its state of formation. If you don't have one, form it this week.
  2. Review how every major asset you own is titled. Identify any that are in your personal name that should be in an entity.
  3. Get an umbrella liability insurance quote. $2M in coverage for under $500 per year is available from most carriers.
