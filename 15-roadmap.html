You've been through the system. Now this is where you put it together into a single, personal plan with milestones, a compound timeline, and a clear picture of where you're going.

The 5-Year Milestone Map

Y1

Foundation Year

LLC active. Business credit started. First digital product launched. WealthPath audit complete. Emergency fund at 3 months. All negative credit items disputed. First net-30 tradelines reporting.

Y2

Income Stack Year

$1,000–$3,000/month in digital product revenue. Business credit score 80+ Paydex. First business credit card approved. S-Corp election filed. Side income covering at least one fixed expense.

Y3

Asset Acquisition Year

First real asset acquired — rental property, land, or business interest. Business line of credit active. Personal credit 720+. Solo 401k funded. Tax strategy running with a qualified CPA.

Y4

Leverage Year

Multiple assets generating cash flow. Holding company structure in place. Business revenue $10K+/month. Umbrella policy active. DSCR loan or seller-financed acquisition closed. Net worth climbing measurably.

Y5

Sovereign Year

Passive income covering core living expenses. Multiple income streams operating. Trust or advanced entity structure in place. Credit score 750+. Net worth $250K–$1M+ depending on starting point and execution speed.
